Spurious‑ish Correlations

Random and strange pairings of Wikipedia pageviews compared over time and against each other.

Data are fetched live from the Wikimedia Pageviews API. These comparisons are for fun and exploration only— correlation does not imply causation.

Sources: Wikimedia Pageviews API. Each card links to the underlying Wikipedia articles.

Built for playful exploration. Time series may visually suggest relationships that aren’t real; the scatter plot and correlation coefficient help reveal whether a linear relationship exists.
